ANA MERCEDES

HOLES

DIGITAL PRINTING

The work of Ana Mercedes Hoyos shows her aesthetic exploration of reality between the end of the last century and the beginning of the present. The exhibition, digital print, presented by the Maja Museum in alliance with the Association of Friends of the National Museum, discovers moments where the influence of POP Art, abstract, geometric and figurative art are present. The luminous tones, the volumes carried to the simplicity of the geometric form, the flat colors, with mixture of shades that determine the depth and the distance, configure the plastic language with which the artist, one of the most important of the national art, assumes in his works the representation of the local color but also of urban and architectural spaces that undoubtedly exerted influence his work.

Ana Mercedes Hoyos travels through different languages of representation and in each of them, present in this exhibition, is its relationship with the environment. Still lifes represent hues and forms that come from the earth. Windows determine their relationship with architecture. In Landscapes the horizon line and color are part of the visible. The almost invisible blue and white tones of The Atmospheres create a minimalist and contemporary space.

The presence of Ana Mercedes Hoyos, one of the most important Colombian artists, is an exceptional occasion to approach her work and a source of pride for the Maja Museum.

Saúl Alvarez Lara
